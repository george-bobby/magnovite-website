General Fest Regulations

Eligibility & Authentication

  • Open to **Undergraduate & Postgraduate students** of all disciplines (National Inter-Collegiate).
  • The fest is **NOT open** to CHRIST (Deemed to be University) students.
  • All participants must carry their **College ID cards** and a Valid ID Proof.

Code of Conduct & Logistics

  • Report to the assigned venue at least **30 minutes prior** to the event start.
  • Ensure event timings **do not overlap** if registered for multiple events.
  • **Immediate disqualification** for unethical practices (plagiarism, vulgarity, obscenity, violence).
  • **Prohibited items** include hazardous/dangerous articles and narcotics, alcohol, or addictive items.
